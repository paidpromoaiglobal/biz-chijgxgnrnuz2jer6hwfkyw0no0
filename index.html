Our Story

Made with Passion

Madurai Meenakshi Mini Mart & Trading Pte Ltd is a trusted Indian grocery store nestled at 54 Buffalo Road, Singapore — the vibrant heart of Little India. We bring you a wide selection of fresh produce, South Indian pantry staples, spices, snacks, and everyday essentials, carefully sourced to meet the needs of Indian families and food lovers across Singapore. Our store is open every day from 8:00 AM to 10:30 PM, so you can shop at your convenience, morning or night.

Rooted in the spirit of Madurai and the warmth of South Indian hospitality, our team is dedicated to making every visit a pleasant and fulfilling experience. Whether you are stocking up on your weekly groceries, looking for that hard-to-find ingredient, or simply popping in for a quick purchase, our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always ready to help. We take pride in offering quality products at fair prices, earning the trust and loyalty of our growing community of satisfied customers.
